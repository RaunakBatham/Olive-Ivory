FAMILY EVENTS

We offer organisation of unforgettable family events in the heart of Palava City. We give to your disposal our Restaurant and the Olive Room, located on the ground floor of the building, as well as two Suite Lounges: The President Lounge and the Ambassador Lounge, located on the 1st and 2nd floor, as well as the Belvedere Room in the Hotel's tower and the Bohema Club at level -1.
