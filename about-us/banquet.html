BANQUET

The Hotel Olive Ivory is an ideal place to organise various celebrations. Depending on your preferences and the number of Guests, we can organise the banquet inside, in the Hotel's internal patio (in a tent) or in another place of your choosing.

We offer a rich, but still affordable menu, which can be adjusted according to the suggestions of our Guests. The reception organised by us can take place with the Guests seated and the meals served by waiters, or in the form of a buffet, where everyone can find something they enjoy.
